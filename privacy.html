Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026 · Last Updated: February 10, 2026

Four Pi Systems LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Stooly mobile application ("Stooly" or the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use Stooly.

We built Stooly with privacy as a core principle. We believe you should understand exactly what happens to your data, and we've designed the App to collect only the minimum information necessary to provide you with a great experience.

The key thing to know: Photos you take in Stooly are used solely for real-time analysis and are never stored. Only the resulting health data (classification, scores, and metrics) is saved to your account.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account using Sign in with Apple, we receive:

  • A unique user identifier provided by Apple
  • Your email address (or Apple's private relay email, if you choose to hide your email)

We do not receive or store your Apple ID password, and we do not collect your name unless you choose to provide it.

1.2 Photos and Camera Access

Stooly requests access to your device's camera to capture photos for analysis. Here is exactly what happens to your photos:

  1. You take a photo within the App.
  2. The photo is transmitted securely to our servers via an encrypted connection (HTTPS).
  3. The photo is sent to a third-party AI processing service for analysis. The AI service processes the image in real-time and returns classification results.
  4. The photo is immediately discarded after analysis. It is never written to disk, stored in a database, or retained in any form on our servers or by the AI service.

We never store, save, view, or share your photos. Period.

1.3 Health and Wellness Data

After AI analysis, the following health-related data points are generated and stored in your account:

  • Bristol Scale classification (stool type 1–7)
  • Color analysis (e.g., brown, dark brown, yellow)
  • Form and consistency descriptors
  • Anomaly flags (e.g., presence of blood, mucus, or other notable characteristics)
  • AI confidence score for the classification
  • Gut Health Score (0–100, calculated from your entries over time)
  • Trend data (aggregated patterns over 30-day periods)
  • Timestamps of each entry

This data is used to provide you with health insights, track trends, and generate personalized wellness tips within the App.

1.4 Subscription and Purchase Data

If you subscribe to Stooly, your purchase is processed through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information (credit card number, billing address, etc.) — this is handled entirely by Apple.

1.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location
  • We do not collect your contacts or address book
  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not use advertising identifiers
  • We do not use tracking pixels or third-party analytics that identify you personally

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Data Purpose
Account info (Apple ID, email) Account creation, authentication, and support communication
Photos (camera) Real-time AI analysis only; immediately discarded
Health data (classifications, scores) Displaying your history, computing your Gut Health Score, generating trends and personalized insights
Subscription status Determining which features you have access to

3. Third-Party Services

Stooly relies on the following third-party services to operate. Each service processes only the minimum data necessary for its function:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Third-party AI service Image analysis and classification Photo (processed in real-time, not retained by the service)
Authentication & database provider User authentication and data storage Account info, health data entries
Cloud hosting provider Server infrastructure Data in transit during API requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Stooly service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers located in the United States. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Encryption at rest: Health data stored in our database is encrypted at rest.
  • Authentication: Your account is protected by secure token-based authentication (JWT) through our authentication provider and Sign in with Apple.
  • Minimal data collection: We only collect and store data that is necessary to provide the service.

While no system can guarantee absolute security, we take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Photos Zero retention. Immediately discarded after real-time analysis. Never written to disk.
Health data (entries, scores, trends) Retained for the lifetime of your active account.
Account information Retained for the lifetime of your active account.
After account deletion All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of your deletion request.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your data: You can view all of your health data at any time within the App.
  • Delete individual entries: You can delete any individual health entry from your history within the App.
  • Delete your account: You can delete your entire account and all associated data from the Settings screen in the App. All data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
  • Request your data: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at support@stooly.app.
  • Revoke camera access: You can revoke camera permissions at any time in your device's Settings. The App will not be able to perform photo analysis without camera access.

7. Children's Privacy

Stooly is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 may have provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email below.

8. International Data Transfers

Our servers and third-party service providers are located in the United States. If you are accessing Stooly from outside the United States, please be aware that your data will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using Stooly, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of Stooly after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
